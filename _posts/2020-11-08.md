Do we have right to flay evils?

![girl_with_pepper](/images/2020-11-08-02-21-05.png)

Recently, A clip came under fire. A man gives a beggar girl a spicy pepper and said "If you eat this, I will give you money instead". Immediately, girl gladly receives it then put that firecrack in her mouth. Although it was so hot that made her cry, she kept smile since this hardship brings her money.

Most of worldwide netizens get mad since they thought it is mere torture. He mocked the girl for the sake of simple fun with the violence called money. But, did the girl think so? Instead of being a clown for a while, she earned money and got strength to live tomorrow. The man got excitement, and girl got money. Isn't it win-win?

![Khabib_pushup](/images/2020-11-08-02-23-36.png)

UFC's lightweight GOAT Habib went through a similar thing, Khabib, for simple fun, asked the homeless on the street for push-ups in exchange for money, then mocked him with his colleagues. Although it doesn't look good, wouldn't it matter if the two sides agreed?

Although the above situations seem morally wrong, the people there were all satisfied, and if it hadn't happened, they would have been a little more unhappy. Nevertheless, should we condemn such actions for the greater good?
