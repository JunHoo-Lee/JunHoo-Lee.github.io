### Generalized Policy Iteration

- On-policy learning
  - Direct experience
  - Learn to estimate and evaluate a policy from experience obtained from following that policy
- Off-policy learning
  - Learn to estimate and evaluate a policy using gathered from following a different policy

PI 는 우리가 model of the world를 알 때 사용.
   
![MC for On policy Q evaluation](/images/2020-11-02-19-34-37.png)

Q를 사용하면 update하기 더 편해진다. 

하지만 어떻게 할 것인가? 만약 state가 deterministic 하다면 episode가 다 똑같을 것이다!
