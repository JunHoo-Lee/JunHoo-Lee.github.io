### How do we guarentee monotonic improvement?

DQN과 다르게, high-stake 문제를 해결하는데 있어서는 중요하다.

#### Conservative Policy Problem.

$$
\pi_{new}(a|s) = (1-\alpha)\pi_{old}(a|s) + \alpha\dot{\pi}(a|s)\\
V^{\pi_{new}} \geq L_{\pi_{old}}(\pi_{new}) - \frac{2\epsilon\gamma}{(1-\gamma)^2}\alpha^2
$$

$$
\epsilon = max_s|\mathbb{E}_{a\sim\pi^\prime(a|s)}[A_\pi(s,a)]|
$$
